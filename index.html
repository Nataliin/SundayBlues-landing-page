THE DISC CLUB

The club's monthly subscription service offers music lovers the opportunity to have exclusive, limited vinyl records delivered to their homes every month.

NEW AND ESTABLISHED ARTISTS

Albums are from established artists as well as new talent, ensuring an eclectic and always exciting music experience. In addition, subscribers have access to exclusive content such as interviews with the artists and Q&A sessions with the curatorial team.

LIMITED AND EXCLUSIVE DISCS

Each subscription includes limited edition vinyl records handpicked by our team of music curators, plus a selection of exclusive goodies such as t-shirts, posters and other surprise goodies.
